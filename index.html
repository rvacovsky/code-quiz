A few things to know before we get started...
1. When you click 'Start', the timer will begin at 75 seconds and you will be presented with your first question.
2. Once you answer your first question you will go on to the next question.
3. If you answer the question incorrectly, time will be subtracted from the clock. (No pressure!)
4. Once you've answered all of the questions the time that is left will be your score!
5. Have fun! Play again to see if you can beat your high score.
